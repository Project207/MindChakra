How It Works
The most simple & convenient way to access talk therapy, Self Healing - anytime, anywhere
Know Your Needs
Explore MindMatters and get to know about the problems you are facing
Take a Mental Health Check Test
"It's okay to not be okay sometimes.” Get open to taking a mental health check to help you get the support you need.
Schedule A Session
Schedule a session with the recommended therapist or talk to a matching expert who will connect you with the right therapist based on your needs.
Get Self-Therapy
In case you don’t need a counsellor, You can even get Self-therapy based on your interest.
Wellness HUB
In addition to the scheduled video or Audio sessions, you can reach out to your therapist or people who are facing same situations as you, via voice messages or chat.
Continuous Support
You have the flexibility to reschedule sessions or change the therapist at any point. For any issues or support, Felicity helpdesk has you covered.
